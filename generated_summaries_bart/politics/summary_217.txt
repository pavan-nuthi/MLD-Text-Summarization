Record numbers of schools across the UK are to take part in a mock general election backed by the government.Pupils in the schools taking part will learn the skills of speech writers, canvassers and political candidates.The Hansard Society, the Electoral Commission and the Department for Education and Skills are running the programme.The mock elections initiative provides an opportunity for pupils to develop their own understanding of how the democratic process works and why it matters.The Y Vote Mock Elections for schools mirror the excitement and buzz of a real election, raising awareness of citizenship, and the benefits of active democracy
