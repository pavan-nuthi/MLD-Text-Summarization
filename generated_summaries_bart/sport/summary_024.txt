Susan Chepkemei has decided she is fit enough to run in next month's Great Edinburgh International Cross Country.The Kenyan was initially unsure if she would have recovered from her gruelling tussle with Paula Radcliffe in the New York Marathon in time to compete.But she has declared herself up to the task and joins a field headed by World cross country champion Benita Johnson."Susan will add even more strength in depth to the world-class line up."
