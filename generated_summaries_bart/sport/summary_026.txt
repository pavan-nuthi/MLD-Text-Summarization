Sprinter Michelle Collins has lodged an appeal against her eight-year doping ban with the North American Court of Arbitration for Sport (CAS).Collins was suspended by the US Anti-Doping Agency based on patterns observed in her blood and urine tests as well as evidence in the Balco investigation.Collins was stripped of her 2003 world and US indoor 200m titles.The 33-year-old received the ban last month as a result of her connection to the federal inquiry into theBalco doping scandal.
