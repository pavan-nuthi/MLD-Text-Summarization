Former government communications chief Alastair Campbell will act as a media consultant to Sir Clive Woodward's 2005 Lions on their tour to New Zealand.Campbell, who left Downing Street earlier this year, will advise on media strategy before and during the tour.The Lions leave for New Zealand on 24 May, with the first Test match against the All Blacks in Christchurch on 25 June.The tour is a massive media event," said Woodward, who will be the head coach."The media and public get the most out of the tour itself," he said.
