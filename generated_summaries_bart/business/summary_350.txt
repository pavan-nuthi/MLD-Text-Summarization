The US dollar has continued its record-breaking slide and has tumbled to a new low against the euro.Investors are betting that the European Central Bank (ECB) will not do anything to weaken the euro, while the US is thought to favour a declining dollar."It's not going to take much to push [the dollar] one way or the other," said Grant Wilson of Mellon Bank.The US currency was trading at $1.364 per euro at 1800 GMT on Monday.
