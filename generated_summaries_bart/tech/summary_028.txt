BBC Sport unveils its new analysis tool Piero at the Wales v England rugby union match on Saturday.But what does it do and how does it work?Picture the scene - Wales are camped on the England line in the dying seconds of the Six Nations' opening match.Piero, named after the Italian painter and pioneer of perspective Piero della Francesco, creates a virtual stadium in which virtual players can be tracked from almost any angle."It allows us to tell the story of a passage of play, tracking individual players across the field, looking at tactics," said Andy Townsend, from BBC Broadcast.BBC
