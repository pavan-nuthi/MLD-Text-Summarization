The 10 authors shortlisted for a Richard and Judy book award in 2005 are hoping for a boost in sales following the success of this year's winner.Richard Madeley and Judy Finnigan's Channel 4 show, is part of the British Book Awards.The TV couple's interest in the book world coined the term "the Richard & Judy effect" and created the top two best-selling paperbacks of 2004 so far.The finalists for 2005 include Andrew Taylor's The American Boy and Robbie Williams' autobiography Feel.
