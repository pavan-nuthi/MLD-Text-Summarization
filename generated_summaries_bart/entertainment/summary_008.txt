The West End is honouring its finest stars and shows at the Evening Standard Theatre Awards in London on Monday.The Producers, starring Nathan Lane and Lee Evans, is up for best musical at the ceremony at the National Theatre.The winner of the Milton Shulman Award for outstanding newcomer will be presented to Dominic Cooper (His Dark Materials and The History Boys), Romola Garai (Calico), Eddie Redmayne (The Goat, or Who is Sylvia?) or Ben Wishaw (Hamlet).Theatre
