Comedy Meet The Fockers topped the festive box office in North America, setting a new record for Christmas Day.The sequel took $44.7m (£23.2m) between 24 and 26 December, according to studio estimates.It took $19.1m (£9.9m) on Christmas Day alone, the highest takings on that day in box office history."When Christmas falls on a weekend, it's bad for business," said Paul Dergarabedian, president of Exhibitor Relations, which compiles box office statistics.
